## 📌 POSITIONING NOTES

- Summary is essential on this resume — without it, recruiters see either a hardware candidate or an AI candidate. The summary establishes the hybrid in 2 sentences.
- "Agentic AI for EDA" in sentence 1 is the ATS unlock — this exact phrase appears in Synopsys, Cadence, and Google JDs as of 2025–2026.
- Keep both sentences — sentence 1 is for ATS and identity; sentence 2 is for the human reader scanning for proof.
- Do NOT say "tape-out" anywhere — the GCN ASIC was completed to GDSII in coursework, not sent to a foundry.
- Do NOT add SOTA comparisons to CVDP — you cannot source the exact benchmark paper in an interview.
- Always keep "zero human intervention" — it makes the CVDP result self-evident and differentiates from candidates who just ran LLMs with human patches.
- GPA 3.81 always — never round to 3.8.
